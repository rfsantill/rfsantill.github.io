For this project, I led the development of a new product to strengthen the company's portfolio in the plastic industry. I researched similar solutions available on the market and identified areas for improvement in our design, which reduced manufacturing costs, shortened product lead time, and enhanced the user experience.

I personally traveled to a plastic customer's facility to meet and understand their product needs firsthand, and establish a partnership to test our prototypes for this product line.

I created the CAD models, drawings, and assembly instructions for the product line. Additionally, I led the internal training meetings for the sales, engineering, manufacturing, and assembly teams to ensure a smooth and successful product launch.
